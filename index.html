Smoothing out Time Series Data

Time series data can be quite noisy, with a lot of up and down spikes. To better see a trend we can plot an average of, say 6 or 12 observations. This is called the rolling mean. We calculate the average in a window of time and move it forward by one observation. Pandas has two handy methods already built in to work this out: rolling() and mean().
